Our Services


EFFECTIVE, JANUARY 1, 2015

• We coordinate and arrange desired, unique funeral services that include an evening visitation prior to the next evening funeral services and next morning. Interment held at the family's cemetery of choice.



• Traditional funeral services include an evening visitation held the day prior to the next days public funeral service. Interment held at the family's cemetery of choice.



• Standard same day funeral services that include a 90 minute visitation and funeral service. Interment held at the family's cemetery of choice.



• Limited funeral services include a chapel visitation, chapel service and same day deposition of remains.



• Basic final glimpse includes non ritualistic formalities. Casket is opened for 60 minutes from opening to closing with 20 minutes designated for open remarks. Same day deposition of remains.



• Gravesite burial services.



• Direct burials and cremations that exclude routine embalming.



• Memorial services designed by the family.



• We are registered participants with Southwest Air Cargo and Delta Air Cargo to receive and ship remains in and out of the state of Missouri.



• Licensed motorcycle funeral escorts are available upon request.
